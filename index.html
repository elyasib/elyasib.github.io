Welcome to my website

Hi, my name is John Rocamora. I hold a BS in Mechatronics Engineering with Highest Honors and a Masters in Biomedical Engineering. I am currently doing a PhD in Biomedical Engineering where I focus on designing, simulating and implementing a variety of algorithms on robotic systems.

My interests lie in studying, designing and implementing biological models for tendon-driven robotics, machine learning algorithms for learning of robot control and stochastic spiking algorihms for risk-aware robot control, among others.
