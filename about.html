About me

My name is Irving Elyasib Santiago Catzim, and I hold a BSc in Mechatronics Engineering from the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM).

I invested a significant part of my academic life designing and developing electronic and computational instruments as part of cutting-edge scientific research. These projects were focused on biophysics, molecular neurosciense, robotics, signal processing, and control.

I have experience as developing back-end systems in financial, banking, and healthcare industries. For the banking industry I have developed software for diverse business areas such as revolving credit, debit and credit cards management, customers information management, and cards embossing.

I am also co-founder of QuetzalCode, a company that provides software and engineering consulting services, providing architecture and design to ensure reliability, security and scalability.
